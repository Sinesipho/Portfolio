About Me


Sinesipho is a Full-Stack Web Developer who loves the thrill of creating something out of nothing through code and transforming ideas into innovative designs to enhance the user’s experience. Her interest in Web Development began when she was introduced to HTML as one of her modules while she was studying towards her qualification in Information Technology (Multimedia) at Tshwane University of Technology. Sinesipho is firm believer in falling in love with the process of becoming the very best version of yourself and celebrating every tiny victory. When she is not programming, she is watching cooking channels or surfing the internet for recipes.
